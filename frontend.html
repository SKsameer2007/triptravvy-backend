🇹🇭 Thailand Package

Thailand — The Ultimate 7-Night Dream Package

By TripTravvy Travel Team  ·  7 Nights / 8 Days  ·  Starting ₹45,999 per person

Thailand is not just a destination — it is a feeling. From the golden spires of Bangkok's temples to the crystal-clear waters of Krabi, this country blends ancient culture with modern luxury like nowhere else on Earth. Our 7-night Thailand package is designed to give you the perfect taste of it all.

"Thailand was always on my bucket list, but TripTravvy made it feel effortless. Every hotel, every transfer, every experience was perfectly arranged." — Priya & Rahul, Mumbai

✈️ Package Highlights

Our Thailand package covers the best of Bangkok, Phuket, Krabi and the iconic Phi Phi Islands. You get 7 nights of handpicked hotel accommodation, daily breakfast, airport transfers, a sunset boat cruise, island-hopping tour, and a local SIM card — everything done for you.

📍 Places You'll Explore

🏙️ BangkokGrand Palace, Wat Pho, Chatuchak Night Market & rooftop bar views of the skyline
🏝️ PhuketPatong Beach, Big Buddha, Bangla Road nightlife & world-famous seafood
🌊 KrabiFour Islands tour, Railay Beach (reachable only by boat), limestone karsts
⛵ Phi Phi IslandsMaya Bay (inspired The Beach film), snorkeling, cliff jumping & bioluminescent waters at night

🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Bangkok → Hotel check-in → Welcome dinner at Asiatique floating market

Day 2: Grand Palace & Wat Pho temple tour → Chao Phraya River cruise → Street food evening

Day 3: Flight to Phuket → Patong Beach afternoon → Bangla Road at night

Day 4: Full-day Phi Phi Islands speedboat tour → Snorkeling at Maya Bay

Day 5: Transfer to Krabi → Railay Beach → Sunset kayaking

Day 6: Four Islands half-day tour → Tiger Cave Temple hike → Evening free

Day 7: Leisure morning → Spa session → Farewell dinner

Day 8: Airport drop-off → Fly home with the best memories of your life

💰 What's Included

✅ 7 nights hotel accommodation (3-star / 4-star options available) · ✅ Daily breakfast · ✅ All airport & inter-city transfers · ✅ Phi Phi Islands speedboat tour · ✅ Four Islands tour (Krabi) · ✅ Local Thailand SIM card · ✅ 24x7 TripTravvy support on WhatsApp

❌ What's Not Included

International airfare · Lunch & dinner (unless specified) · Personal shopping · Travel insurance (recommended — available at extra cost)
